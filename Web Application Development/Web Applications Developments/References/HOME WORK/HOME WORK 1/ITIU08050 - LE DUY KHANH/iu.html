My Experiences

I am student currently attending my third year at a International University - HCM city. It is a respectible university, at least here, and am overall satisfied with my choice - both of university and major.

I've learned much in the courses I've attended in IU. There are many courses in how to program in a particular language, several in how to write algorithms for solving a particular problem, as well as one in how to program under pressure, but none in how to think about programming and how to approach problems in an efficient manner. Also, I encounter many students who have never concerned themselves with C or C++: languages heavily used in industry.
